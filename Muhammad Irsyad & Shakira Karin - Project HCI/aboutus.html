Our History

VOrgreens has built a reputation for exceptional cuisine, excellent service, delicious, healthy food, fair rates, and a pleasant ambiance since its inception in 2017.

VOrgreens is a plant-based eatery chain in Indonesia that serves delicious, healthy, ethical, and sustainable food founded by two Binus University students, Irsyad and Karin. VOrgreens offers a wide variety of healthy and delectable cuisines.

VOrgreens began as a tiny shop, but with time, it has grown into a restaurant with a welcoming atmosphere. VOrgreen currently offers a variety of menus, ranging from main courses, drinks, and side dishes to desserts, all of which are both delicious and nutritious.

Our Vision and Mission

To shape the future of exceptionally good foods for people all over the world and to bring joy to our customers through high-quality, nutritious food.

We will try to provide food goods that are both delicious and healthy, as we are concerned about the nutritional value of each product we provide.
